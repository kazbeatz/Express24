SCHOOL CLEANING

It is of the highest priority to have a clean and healthy environment for our children. That is why here at Express24clean we have trained our cleaners to perform school cleaning services to the highest standard. There are all sorts of nasty bacteria that can be of danger to children, therefore it is vital that the environment is sanitized regularly.

All of our cleaners are interviewed in person, tested and fully-vetted by our founder before they are offered one-to-one training by one of our supervisors. Only when we are confident in our new cleaners we start allocating them to the jobs.

Economical and Safe School Cleaning Services

At Cleaning Express we clean schools and educational bodies of all sizes including nurseries, pre-schools, primary schools, secondary schools, colleges, universities and city academies. An educational setting whether it is a school or university requires a high standard of cleaning and trustworthy, reliable cleaners are essential. We know that every school, college or university is different and we take time to draw an individual plan to cover every aspect of our work before we start cleaning.

Child protection is of paramount importance to us and we use designated cleaners for nursery and school cleaning. We tailor our cleaning rotas around the requirements of your school meaning that your staff and pupils will not be affected by the cleaning taking place. We also offer handyman services, painting, decorating & deep clean services during holidays.

We follow governments advice on coronavirus and our staff have strict COVID-19 guidelines on following social distancing, keeping the surfaces clean and self-isolating if necessary.
